Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This page explains, in plain language, what data Doinik Life ("we", "us") collects, why, where it is stored, and what rights you have. We try to keep it short.

1. What we collect

  • Account data — your email address and a password hash, used by Firebase Authentication to sign you in.
  • App data — the tasks, expenses, water/mood entries, loans, bazar items, and orders you create. This is the data you intentionally save into the app.
  • Local preferences — small settings (language, theme, last-used tab) stored in your browser's localStorage on your device.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not embed Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar.

3. Where your data lives

Your account and app data are stored in Google Firebase (Authentication + Realtime Database). Firebase encrypts data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Your data is logically scoped to your account, and only requests authenticated as your account can read or write your data.

4. How we use your data

We use your data only to provide the Doinik Life service to you — i.e., to show you your own tasks, expenses, etc. We do not analyse it for advertising or profile-building.

5. Cookies & similar storage

Doinik Life uses your browser's localStorage for app state, and Firebase Authentication uses cookies/tokens to keep you signed in. We do not set marketing cookies.

6. Your rights

  • You can view all your data inside the app.
  • You can delete any individual entry.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data — email hello@doinik.life with the subject "Delete my account" and we will action it within 7 days.

7. Children

Doinik Life is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, please email us so we can remove the account.

8. Changes

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "last updated" date above and, where reasonable, notify you in-app.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: hello@doinik.life.
